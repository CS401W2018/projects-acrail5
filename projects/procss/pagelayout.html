What is the One Piece?

This Anime is about a main character named Monkey D Luffy wanting to become the King of Pirates. He sails out to become the strongest with his crewmembers seeking out to find the One Piece. The One Piece is the final treasure which will make you the King of Pirates. Monkey D Luffy and his crew all becomes stronger as they fight other pirates out at sea.

The crew members are Monkey D Luffy the captain, Zoro the swordman, Sanji the cook, Nami the navigator, Brook the musician, Chopper the doctor, Franky the builder, and Robin the wisdom. They all seek to find the All Blue or One Piece. One Piece is an amazing anime to watch if you are willing to also seek an adventure yourself by watching all 1500+ episodes. They are about 24 minutes each.
