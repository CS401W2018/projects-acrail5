Hajime No Ippo

I like this Anime because its about a young boy who gets bullied and decides to join into the boxing life style. The main character name is Ippo and he is an inspiration to many boxing fans.

Solo Leveling

This anime is about a boy who goes into dungeons to earn money to cure his sickly mother. He gets stronger and hides his powers. I like this anime because theres a lot of action and ruthless fighting scenes.

One Piece

This is one of my favorite anime because theres a lot of adventures and stories being told in all 1000+ episodes. Pirates trying to become the king of the pirates.
