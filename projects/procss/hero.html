My Hero - Ippo Makunouchi

Interests Hero

Ippo Makunouchi is the main character in the series Hajime No Ippo. He is a high school student who started boxing to find out what it means to be strong. He lost his father at a young age and lives with his mother helping out the family fishing business. He was bullied and rescued by a professional boxer, which inspired Ippo to pursue in boxing. He is known for his powerful punches which lead him to gain many achievements.

Japan Rookie King
His first win to becoming a professional boxer.
All Japan Rookie King
His second win demonstrating his path to a pro boxer.
JBC Featherweight Championship
A title he defended multiple times throughout his career.
