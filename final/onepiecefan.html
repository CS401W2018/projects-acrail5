The One Piece

One Piece is a popular anime and manga series created by Eiichiro Oda. This anime tells a story about the adventures of Monkey D. Luffy and his crew as they search for the ultimate treasure: the One Piece.

This fanpage is dedicated to exploring the characters, story arcs, and interesting facts about the world of One Piece.

5 facts bout Eiichiro Oda

  1. He has been married to Chiaki Inaba since 2004.
  2. Oda is known for his intense work ethic, often sleeping only three hours per night.
  3. He received the Guinness World Record in 2022 for the copies of One Piece.
  4. Oda decided to become a manga artist at the age of four to avoid having a "real job".
  5. He is the creator of One Piece, the best-selling manga series in history, with over 520 million copies.
